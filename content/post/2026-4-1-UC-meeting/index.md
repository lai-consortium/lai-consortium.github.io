Many thanks to all participants for the fruitful discussion

<!--more-->

In March 2026, we held our fourth User Committee Meeting. During this meeting we discussed different tool design alternatives and their potential impact on clinical practice. This discussion allowed us to get valuable feedback from relevant experts in different domains. This input will help us on our path towards developing a clinically impactful liver lesion phenotyping tool.

Many thanks to Fredrik Illerstam, Maarten de Gouw, Cheryl Roumen, Joris Erdmann, Siyar Kisin, Gaukhar Medetova, Federica Vernuccio, Roberto Cannella, Jip Prince, and Dave Sprengers.
